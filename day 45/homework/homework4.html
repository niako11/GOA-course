Porsche 911

Information

The Porsche 911 model series is a family of German two-door, high performance rear-engine sports cars, introduced in September 1964 by Porsche AG of Stuttgart, Germany. Now in its eighth generation, all 911s have a rear-mounted flat-six engine, usually 2+2 seating, except for special 2-seater variants.
